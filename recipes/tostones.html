Tostones

Tostones are the yellow slices that are on the plate. They are plantaions cut on an angle and twice fried.

This is a popular latin american dish served as a side or appitizer

Ingredients

  • Green plantains
  • Pot full of oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Pique
  • Pot
  • knife
  • Kitchen Rack
  • paper towels

Steps

  1. Soak plantains in warm water for 5 minutes
  2. Slice Plantains along spine and peel
  3. Cut two inch slices diagonally
