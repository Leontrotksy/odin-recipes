Tostones

Tostones are the yellow slices that are on the plate. They are plantaions cut on an angle and twice fried.

This is a popular latin american dish served as a side or appitizer

Ingredients

  • Green plantains
  • Pot full of oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Pique
  • Pot
  • knife
  • Kitchen Rack
  • paper towels

Steps

  1. Soak plantains in warm water for 5 minutes
  2. Slice Plantains along spine and peel
  3. Cut two inch slices diagonally
  4. Set pot of oil to high
  5. Once a wooden spoon fizzes in the oil put slices to fry
  6. remove once browned and place on rack above paper towels to dry
  7. Once slices cool smash the slices flat
  8. Fry the slices for several minutes
  9. Place to cool and dry and salt and pepper
  10. once cool put on pique and eat
